BIOGRAPHY

Chukwuani Kingsley Odinaka was born on October 10, 1993, in Enugu State, Nigeria. He is a Nigerian, a computer instructor and student. Odinaka grew up in Enugu State where he attended Mother Mary Primary school and National Grammar School for his secondary education. He graduated from Enugu State University Of Science And Technology in 2018 with a degree in Agronomy and Ecological Management.

After graduating from University, Odinaka did his NYSC at Katsina State (Northern part of Nigeria) between 2019-2020. Thereafter, Odinaka began working as a computer instructor at Federal Government College Enugu under Cisco Networking Academy. He is also a student of Altschool Africa, where he is studying Software Engineering.

Odinaka is passionate about technology and education. He believes that everyone should have access to quality education and training, especially in the field of technology He is also committed to helping others learn and grow. In his spare time, Odinaka enjoys surfing the internet, reading and travelling. He is also a member of his church choir.
